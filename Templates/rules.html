How a Game Works:

The game of Say More relies upon your expertise as a communicator - explain the concept without using the name of the concept.
While the premise is simple, the tasks can be deceptively challenging as your ability to explain the content depends on both your knowledge of the content and your verbal articulation. You might know what is being referenced, but can you explain it to someone else?
